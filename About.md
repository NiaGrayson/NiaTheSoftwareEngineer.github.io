# About Me

  I'm a Technology Professional with over four years of experience solving complex technical problems in enterprise and managed service environments. Over the past four years, I have built my career in enterprise IT and managed service environments, where every day has presented new opportunities to troubleshoot complex technical issues, improve business operations, and deliver dependable solutions that help users stay productive.
  
  My foundation in IT support has given me experience working across a wide range of technologies, including Microsoft 365, Active Directory, Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD), Microsoft Intune, Windows administration, networking, and enterprise service management platforms like ServiceNow. These experiences have strengthened not only my technical skills but also my ability to communicate effectively, think critically under pressure, and approach problems from both a technical and business perspective. One of the aspects of technology I enjoy most is that there is always something new to learn. I am motivated by the process of understanding how systems work and finding ways to make them work better. 
  
  Throughout my career, I have consistently ranked among the top-performing support specialists by delivering high-quality technical support while collaborating with cross-functional teams in fast-paced environments. Those experiences have reinforced the importance of teamwork, adaptability, and maintaining a customer-first mindset. As my career has evolved, so has my curiosity. Supporting enterprise environments sparked a deeper interest in the software that powers the systems I work with every day. Instead of simply maintaining technology, I want to contribute to building it.
  
  Today, I am expanding my expertise into software engineering, combining my real-world experience with modern development practices to build applications that are scalable, intuitive, and impactful. I enjoy creating clean, efficient code, learning new technologies, and developing solutions that bridge technical challenges with business needs. Over the years, I have consistently delivered high-quality technical support, ranked among the top-performing support specialists, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve complex issues in fast-paced environments.   
  
  This portfolio serves as a reflection of my growth as a technology professional and my commitment to continuous learning. My passion for expanding my knowledge and contributions to the IT field is was drives me to succeed. I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and contribute to projects that create meaningful impact. Follow my journey, as I approach each opportunity with curiosity, persistence, and a commitment to continuous improvement.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope it provides insight into the work I am passionate about, and the direction I am headed as I continue building a career in software engineering!🤗
